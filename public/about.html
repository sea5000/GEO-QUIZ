About

Hi I'm Spencer, a student at Modul University in Vienna, Austria. My project partner Ella and I are conducting a little study for a Math and Statistics class project. I was greatly invested and built this website (I'm relatively new to WEBDEV), so watch out for any bugs.

The study is 40 questions, 20 for each the U.S. and Europe randomly chosen. The goal of the survey is to compare knowledge of states and European countries in order to test the notion that Americans don't know European geography and vice versa. (See tiktok)
